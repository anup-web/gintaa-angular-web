Specific Terms

A. Refund of Courier Charges

Whenever, you are using any courier facility arranged by gintaa, you shall have to make certain payment towards the courier unless categorically exempted.

While, courier charges once paid shall not be refundable, there may be some exceptional situation where any part or whole of the courier charges paid by you is entitled for refund. In such a situation, the Refund shall be initiated as under:-

Payment Mode Refund to Timeline
Credit Card / Debit Card The Credit Card / Debit Card Account from where it was paid 5-7 business days
Net Banking Account Credited to Bank Account from where it was paid. 5-7 business days
UPI Linked Bank Account Credited to Bank Account from where it was paid. 5-7 business days
Payment Wallet Credited to the e-Wallet from where it was paid. 1-2 business days

C. Refund of Money Paid for Returned Items

I. For Exchange of Used Items:

As the payment made by you will be credited directly to the account of the seller, please read the exchange or refund rules proposed by your seller and trade accordingly. gintaa shall not be responsible for any transaction under this category except to the extent that of providing a platform.

II. For Sale of New Items by gintaa registered sellers:

While concluding a sale please read and understand the return and refund policy of the specific item or category. While majority items shall be returnable and thus entitled for a refund, some of them might not be returnable, like perishable items, innerwear, medical equipment etc. and thus not entitled to refund.

For products, which are returnable and thus entitled to refund shall be subject to the following refund policy.

You shall generally have 15 days’ time to refund unless specifically modified by the seller at the time of sale.

You shall have to initiate the return within those 15 days for being picked up by our authorised representative.

Soon after we receive the product you return or the seller confirms gintaa about the receipt of return, as the case may be, a refund is issued to the original payment method or to your bank account or b way of cheque despatched to your registered addressed, as per the following timeline:-
