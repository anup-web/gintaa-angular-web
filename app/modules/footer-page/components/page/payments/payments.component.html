Payments

gintaa brings you a secured and encrypted payment platform to help you buy/sell and barter your products here. Therefore, we are going live with payment options only for buying and selling products.

What are the payment options available on gintaa?

The payment options are available on gintaa include internet banking, credit/debit cards, UPI, etc.

How can I withdraw my funds from gintaa?

gintaa has a strict policy for storing the money for any transactions for 45 days and will only release the payments to the sellers via bank transfer. Therefore, you need to provide appropriate valid bank account details or UPI details to withdraw funds from gintaa. In case you need to add your bank account information, please add it from the “My accounts” section.

Are there any hidden charges applicable on gintaa?

No, gintaa doesn’t charge any additional charges when you make any purchase on gintaa. However, always check the details of your transactions to avoid any errors.

What happens if my payment fails?

Contact gintaa support immediately to know about your payment status. Also, in case of payment failure, if any funds are debited from your account, then it will be reverted to your bank account in 5-7 days. Please contact your bank for more details.

Do you offer any product on cash on delivery?

Currently, we aren’t offering any cash on delivery for any transactions on gintaa. But we hope to launch it soon for you.
